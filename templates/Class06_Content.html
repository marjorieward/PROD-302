Before Class:

  • Bring the following pattern pieces (cut, notched, and marks transferred) to class:
    • Reverse Applique Pattern
    • Backing Pattern (1 Piece)
  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • 1 sheet of craft felt
    • Juki Industrial Bobbin
    • Juki Industrial Bobbin Case
    • DBx1 sewing needles 90/14
    • Hand Sewing Needles
    • All-purpose thread in desired color(s)
    • Medium weight muslin
    • Solid or printed cotton fabric
    • Weft Interfacing
    • Clear ruler with 1/8 inch markings
    • Frixion brand pen(s)
    • Ball head quilter's pins
    • Fabric Scissors
    • Paper Scissors
    • Transfer Paper
    • Serrated Transfer Wheel
    • Seam Ripper
    • Tape Measure
    • Thread snips/embroidery scissors (optional)

During Class:

  • Students practice with the following samples:
    • Reverse applique
    • Joined felt shapes

After Class:

  • Complete the quilting, trapunto, corded trapunto, reverse applique, and joined felt shapes samples and bring them to our next class meeting.
  • Bring your Riches to Rags dress to our next class meeting.
