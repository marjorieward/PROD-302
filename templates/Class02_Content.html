Before Class:

  • Bring the following pattern pieces to class:
    • Knife Pleats Pattern
    • Box Pleats Pattern
    • Pin Tucks Pattern
    • Tuck and Fold Pattern
  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Juki Industrial Bobbin
    • Juki Industrial Bobbin Case
    • DBx1 sewing needles 90/14
    • Hand Sewing Needles
    • All-purpose thread in desired color(s)
    • Medium weight muslin
    • Clear ruler with 1/8 inch markings
    • Frixion brand pen(s)
    • Ball head quilter's pins
    • Fabric Scissors
    • Paper Scissors
    • Transfer Paper
    • Serrated Tracing Wheel
    • Seam Ripper
    • Tape Measure
    • Thread snips/embroidery scissors (optional)

During Class:

  • Students practice various forms of pleating and pin tucks:
    • Knife pleats
    • Box pleats
    • Pin tucks
    • Tuck and Fold

After Class:

  • Complete pleating and pin tuck samples and bring them to our next class meeting.
