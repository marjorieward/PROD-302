Before Class:

  • Complete the Pre-Quarter Assignment
  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Hand Sewing Needles
    • All-purpose thread in desired color(s)
    • Fabric Scissors
    • Paper Scissors
    • Thread snips/embroidery scissors (optional)

During Class:

  • Practice threading and operation of the industrial lockstitch machine and serger.
  • Begin cutting fabric for sample book.

After Class:

  • Print or copy provided patterns and cut all necessary pattern pieces following the pattern cutting instructions.
